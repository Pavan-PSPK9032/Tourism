The Ruins of Hampi

Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site located in Karnataka, is renowned for its ancient temples, palaces, and captivating landscapes. Once the capital of the Vijayanagara Empire, Hampi is a treasure trove of history and architecture.

Some of the must-visit sites include the Virupaksha Temple, Vittala Temple, and the iconic stone chariot. The surreal boulder-strewn landscape, combined with rich cultural heritage, makes Hampi a must-visit destination for history enthusiasts and adventure seekers.
