The Gem of the East Coast

Andhra Pradesh, located on the southeastern coast of India, is known for its diverse cultural heritage, beautiful beaches, and historic temples. The state is home to the famous Tirumala Venkateswara Temple, one of the most visited pilgrimage sites in the world.

The pristine beaches of Visakhapatnam, the rich historical past of Amaravati, and the scenic beauty of Araku Valley make Andhra Pradesh a delightful destination for travelers. The state's cuisine, known for its tangy and spicy flavors, is another highlight of any visit.
