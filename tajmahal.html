The Symbol of Love

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, is one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, this white marble monument attracts millions of visitors from across the globe.
